About Us

We look forward for bigger aims.

CEO, Kavilov Pulat

"Local Textile Brand" LLC (LTB) was founded by Kavilov Pulat in 2019 in the Jizzakh region of the Republic of Uzbekistan, which is about 170 miles from Tashkent.

The company specializes in producing knitted fabric and cloth of different kinds

Some important Feature

  • The company has 40 knitting machines of popular international brands such as TERROT (ITALY), WELLTEX (CHINA), HONGI (CHINA).
  • Maximum capacity of each knitting machine is 700-750 kg of knitted fabric.
  • The firm produces over 3000 tons of knitted fabric a year.
  • Three main materials fabric is made of are Cotton Yarns, Pallister Yarns and Splandex Yarns.
  • Over 70% of the production is sold and distributed locally in the Uzbek market and about 30% of the goods is exported mainly to Turkey, Bangladesh, India, Pakistan and Tajikistan.
  • LTB is increasing its exporting capacity and is ready to supply more knitted fabric to Turkey
